City of Leavenworth!

Seeking German-inspired cuisine, breathtaking mountain views, and Bavarian-style architecture? Look no further! Leavenworth is a charming city known for its plethora of wineries, restaurants and shops. Whether you enjoy river rafting, hiking, or site seeing during the warm seasons or enjoy a winter wonderland full of activities such as sledding, snowmobiling, showshoeing and even Christmas carolling, Leavenworth is the city for you.

Leavenworth is a small city in Chelan County region. It is part of the Wenatchee-East Wenatchee Metropolitan Statistical Area, and has a population of 2,491(2024 cencus), according to World Population Review. Leavenworth is classified as an urban suburban mixed city, and was incorporated in 1906 - making it 118 years old! According to DATA USA, Leavenworth boasts an average income of $60,982 annually. In comparison, it falls significantly short of Washington's average income of $82,400, by about $21,418.

Leavenworth hosts several annual festivals, including: Oktoberfest, which is located downtown Leavenworth. It is full of music, dancing, and ceremonial tapping of the kegs! There is also a 62-foot Ferris wheel, bouncy obstacle course, root beer garden, face painting, games and many more activities for all ages.



Maifest is the traditional German celebration of the arrival of Spring. The Maypole dance is the most popular Maifest tradition. It involves raising a tall wooden pole(typically decorated with ribbons and flowers) in the center of town. People then dance around the Maypole, weaving the ribbons into a pattern around the pole. Autumn Leaf Festival celebrates the turning colors of the autumn leaves. This event turns the town into a thriving tourist destination! There are food booths, live music in the park, the kids parade, and the Grand Parade. Leavenworth is a great destination for visitors and their families.

Leavenworth is home to the Leavenworth Nutcracker Museum, which opened in 1995, and contains more than 9,000 nutcrackers, dating back to the 16th and 17th centuries. Visitors can also find ornaments, books, collectibles, smokers, and souvenirs at the gift shop.

Leavenworth has many guided tours for visitors. The Bavarian Walking Tour is a magical experience through Leavenworth. This tour is for all ages! Here you can learn about the history of Leavenworth, see amazing landmarks, and you can even bring your pet with you!
